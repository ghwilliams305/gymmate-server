Welcome to Gymmate — Your Personal Fitness Planning Companion

Gymmate is built for students, beginners, and everyday athletes who want a smarter, simpler way to plan workouts, track progress, and stay consistent. Whether you're discovering Gymmate for the first time or returning to check your dashboard, our mission stays the same: make fitness clearer, easier, and fully in your control.

Your Data, Your Control

We believe your fitness data belongs to you. That’s why Gymmate is designed with transparency and user control at the core.

Here’s the type of information Gymmate may store once you create an account and begin using the app:

  • Account Details — Your name, email, and authentication ID (e.g., Google Sign-In).
  • Preferences — Fitness goals, settings, and any custom preferences you set.
  • Activity — Exercises, sets, reps, weights, workout history, and progress metrics.
  • Information — Device type, app version, and performance logs that help us improve app functionality.
  • Optional Inputs — Notes, targets, or additional health/fitness data you choose to share.

Full Transparency, Always

Once logged in, users can access a dedicated Data Dashboard, where you can:

  • View everything Gymmate has collected about your account
  • Search through your stored workout history
  • Download your data for your own records
  • Request deletion of specific data or your entire profile

We want the Gymmate experience to be trustworthy and empowering — a place where your progress stays private, secure, and yours.
